The Texas Method

Monday: Volume day

  1. Squat 5×5 at 90% of 5RM
  2. Bench Press (A) / Overhead Press (B) 5×5 at 90% of 5RM
  3. Deadlift 1×5 at 90% of 5RM

Wednesday: Recovery/variety day

  1. Squat 2×5 at 80% of Monday's work weight
  2. Overhead Press (A) / Bench Press (B) 3×5 at 90% of last Monday's weight
  3. Back Extension or Glute-Ham Raise 5×10
  4. Chin-up 3×bodyweight to failure

Friday: High-intensity day

  1. Squat, work up to new 5RM
  2. Bench Press (A) / Overhead Press (B), work up to new 5RM
  3. Power Clean 5×3 or Power Snatch 6×2

Misc.

1RM = w·(30 + r)/30
